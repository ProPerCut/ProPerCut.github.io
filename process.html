THE CRAFT

How I Engineer
High-Retention Videos

Editing is not about cuts. It’s about controlling emotion, pacing and attention — frame by frame.

01

Script & Hook Analysis

Every edit begins with understanding the hook. I restructure openings to secure attention within the first 5–8 seconds.

02

Emotional Beat Mapping

I map emotional highs and lows to avoid flat pacing and design moments where viewers subconsciously lean in.

03

Visual Rhythm Design

Cuts, zooms, b-roll and motion are timed to rhythm — not randomly — to prevent viewer fatigue.

04

Sound & Silence Control

Sound design isn’t background noise. Strategic silence and hits guide emotional response.

05

Retention Checkpoints

Every 4–6 seconds, I introduce a visual or auditory reset to reduce drop-off and extend watch time.

This Is Why My Edits Don’t Feel Random

Each frame has a purpose. Each second earns the next.
